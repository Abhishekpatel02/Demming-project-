Family Thearapy

Regardless of the origin of the situation, and regardless of the weather you consider it an "individual" of "family" issue, involving families in solutions is often beneficial. This involvement of families is commonly accomplished by their direct participation in the thearapy session. The skills of family thearapist thus include the ability of influence conversations in a way that catalyzes the strength, wisdom and support of family

Family thearapy uses a range of counseling and other techniques including:

  • Communication-theory
  • Psychotharapy
  • Systematic Coaching
  • Psychoeducation
  • Relationship Education
  • Systems Theory
